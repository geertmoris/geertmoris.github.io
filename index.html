About me...

Now 60 year old, I can boast a wealth of experience, both in work and in life.

I have been active in a wide variety of sectors and industries and I found them all fascinating. Together with this, technical stuff and programming have always been important occupations.

I started in the early eighties with 'Basic' and later versions such as 'GW-Basic'. After that I focused more on VBA in combination with Excel sheets. In this I wrote a lot of applications that I used both for my job and my hobbies.

Recently I followed the training for Java developer at the VDAB and now I get the chance to apply this in practice. I am really looking forward to it.
